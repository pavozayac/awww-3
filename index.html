Elden Ring Bosses

Elden Ring hosts a great number of bosses, who have varying mechanics and difficulty. This website provides a totally accurate description of some of the bosses.

Margit, the Fell Omen

Margit, the Fell Omen is one of the first bosses you meet in Elden Ring. He is a large, humanoid boss with a sword-like staff. He has a variety of attacks, including a charge, a slam, and a spin attack. Even though he is a relatively easy boss his attacks tend to be misleadingly telegraphed and provoke many beginners to dodge excessively, only to end up caught by surprise and defeated.

Margit's story
Margit, the Fell Omen, is a powerful sorcerer who has been corrupted by the power of the Elden Ring. He was once a wise and benevolent ruler, but the power of the ring has twisted him into a malevolent force. He now seeks to spread chaos and destruction throughout the lands, and it is up to the player to stop him.
